# Common questions

Here are the questions we get asked most. If yours isn't here, email us at [support@auctionball.com](mailto:support@auctionball.com).

## Setting up

> **My organization name is taken. What do I do?**
> Try a slightly different subdomain — for example, "bpl-cup-2026" instead of "bpl-cup". The organization name itself can be repeated; only the subdomain (the web address) needs to be unique across all of AuctionBall.

> **Can I run multiple tournaments at the same time?**
> On Free and Starter plans, only one season is "active" at a time. On Pro and Enterprise plans, you can have unlimited seasons in parallel. (You can still have many seasons stored on Starter / Free — only one drives the live auction page at any moment.)

> **I changed my mind — can I delete my tournament and start over?**
> Yes. Click your name at the top-right → Settings → Delete organization. Type your subdomain to confirm. Everything is deleted permanently — no recovery — so export anything you want to keep first.

## Money

> **Why does my bid step show as $9 when I set 1000?**
> You set the **BDT bid step** to 1000 but your display currency is set to USD. The two are independent. Either switch to BDT mode, or set a separate **USD bid step** on the season (try 10 or 25, whatever feels right).

> **A team's budget went negative — how?**
> It can't, actually. Bids that exceed remaining budget are rejected at the moment they're placed. If you see negative on a team, edit their initial budget upward by the same amount.

> **Can I run my auction in dollars in Bangladesh?**
> Yes. Settings → Display currency → USD. Set the conversion rate. Everything throughout the app — base prices, budgets, bids — shows in dollars from then on.

## Auction night

> **The big screen isn't updating in real time.**
> Check the connection dot at the top-right of the big-screen header. If it's red, your internet is down or unstable. Refresh the page; if that doesn't help, switch to a wired internet connection or a different Wi-Fi network.

> **My auctioneer's "Start" button does nothing.**
> They haven't picked a player yet. They need to click a player from the left queue first.

> **Auto-finalize sold a player while I was looking away — can I undo it?**
> Yes. Click the player → click **Reset**. The bids are cleared, the buying team's budget is refunded, and the player goes back to the queue.

> **One team's phone keeps showing "offline".**
> Their phone is having internet trouble. Have them refresh the page or switch from mobile data to Wi-Fi (or vice versa). The big-screen and other phones won't be affected.

## Player registration

> **Players say the form is asking for things I don't need.**
> You can customize the form. Seasons → Form builder → remove or reorder fields. Built-in fields (name, photo, category, etc.) can't be removed, but everything else is up to you.

> **Submitted players don't appear in my players list.**
> Check the **Pending** filter at the top of the Players page. Public registrations need your approval before they enter the auction queue.

> **Can I require players to upload a photo of their NID / passport?**
> Yes. Form builder → add an **Image upload** field, set the size (e.g., 600×600), make it required.

> **The phone number showing on my registration page is wrong.**
> That comes from the **Registration instructions** field on the season. Edit it and save — Seasons → Public registration → instructions.

## Billing

> **My bKash payment hasn't been approved after 24 hours.**
> Email [support@auctionball.com](mailto:support@auctionball.com) with your transaction ID. We'll check immediately.

> **My subscription expired but my tournament is tomorrow — help!**
> Email support. We can extend your access manually while you complete payment.

## Login and accounts

> **I forgot my password.**
> On the login screen, click "Forgot password?" — type your email, we send a reset link. The link is valid for 60 minutes.

> **My invitation link expired.**
> Invitations last 14 days. Ask the person who invited you to send a fresh one.

> **I'm a team owner but I can see admin tools — that's wrong, right?**
> Probably your role was set to "Admin" by mistake when you were invited. Ask the organizer to revoke and re-invite you with the correct role.

## Language

> **How do I switch between Bengali and English?**
> Top-right of the dashboard, next to your profile photo. Click the language toggle. Your choice is remembered for next time.

> **Is the player registration form bilingual?**
> Field labels translate when the player switches languages on the form, but anything you typed manually (custom field labels, instructions) stays in whatever language you typed it.

## Still stuck?

Email [support@auctionball.com](mailto:support@auctionball.com). Include:

- Your subdomain (e.g., bpl-cup-2026.auctionball.com)
- What you were trying to do
- A screenshot if possible

We usually reply within a few hours during Bangladesh business hours.
