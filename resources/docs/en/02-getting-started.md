# Getting started

This is the 5-minute path from "I just heard about AuctionBall" to "my account is ready and I can start adding teams."

## Step 1 — Sign up

1. Open the home page and click **Get started**
2. Fill in:
   - **Tournament / organization name** — for example, "Dhaka Premier League 2026" or "BPL Cricket Cup"
   - **Subdomain** — a short web address for your tournament. If you type "bpl-cup", your link becomes `bpl-cup.auctionball.com`. Use lowercase letters, numbers, and dashes only.
   - **Your name and email** — this becomes your admin account
   - **Password** — pick something strong (at least 8 characters)
   - **Plan** — start with **Free** while you're setting up. You can upgrade any time without losing your data.
3. Click **Create organization**

That's it — you're now logged in and on the dashboard.

## Step 2 — Pick your money: BDT or USD

By default, every price, bid, and budget shows in Bangladeshi Taka (৳). If your tournament uses US Dollars instead:

1. Click **Settings** in the left sidebar
2. Under **Display currency**, pick **USD**
3. Type the conversion rate (how many ৳ in one $) — for example, 110
4. Click **Save**

From this moment, everything in the app shows in your chosen currency. You can switch back to BDT any time and your data stays the same.

## Step 3 — Pick your sport when you make your first season

A "season" is one tournament — for example "BPL 2026 Spring Cup". You'll create your first season in the next guide. When you do, pick **cricket** or **football** — the form fields adapt automatically (cricket asks for batting/bowling style, football doesn't).

## What's already done for you

When you signed up, AuctionBall automatically:

- Created an empty season for the current year (you can rename it or replace it)
- Set your plan limits (Free plan: 1 season, 20 players, 4 teams)
- Picked your language based on your browser

## Common questions before you start

> **Do I need to know anything technical?**
> No. Everything is point-and-click. The only "uploading" you'll do is your team logos and player photos.

> **Can I have multiple admins?**
> Yes. You can invite as many co-admins as you like. See [Inviting your team](/help/roles-and-permissions).

> **What if I outgrow the Free plan?**
> Upgrade any time from **Billing** in the sidebar. Pay through bKash (send to a number, paste the transaction ID) or PayPal. Your players, teams, and history stay exactly where they are.

> **Can I delete my account if I change my mind?**
> Yes. From **Settings** you can delete the whole organization. We don't ask any questions.
