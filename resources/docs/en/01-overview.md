# Welcome to AuctionBall

AuctionBall lets you run a real player auction for your cricket or football tournament — like the BPL or IPL, but for any tournament, big or small. The auctioneer drives the bidding from a laptop, team owners place bids from their phones, and everyone watches the live action on a TV or projector.

## What you can do with AuctionBall

- **Run a live player auction** with a stopwatch, bidding teams, and a big screen for the audience
- **Set budgets per team** and watch them spend in real time
- **Let players sign up online** through a custom form you design (collect their photo, jersey number, contact, payment proof — whatever you need)
- **Accept registration fees** through bKash, Nagad, Rocket, bank transfer, or PayPal
- **Use it in Bengali or English** — switch any time from the language toggle

## How long does it take to set up?

About 30 minutes from sign-up to your first practice auction:

- 5 min — create your account and tournament
- 10 min — add 4-8 teams with budgets
- 10 min — add 20-50 players (or share a sign-up link and let them register themselves)
- 5 min — practice run with the live auction page

## A quick mental model

Three people use AuctionBall during a live auction:

1. **The auctioneer** sits at a laptop with the **Live auction** page open. They pick the next player, start the timer, watch bids come in, and click "Sold" or "Unsold" when it's done.
2. **The team owners** each watch a phone with the **bidding page** open. They tap a big button to bid for the player on the block.
3. **The audience** watches a TV or projector showing the **Big screen** — same data as the auctioneer, but huge fonts and dramatic animations.

All three stay in sync automatically. The auctioneer doesn't need to refresh anything; the phones don't need to refresh; the TV doesn't need to refresh. Whatever happens on one screen shows up on the others within a second.
