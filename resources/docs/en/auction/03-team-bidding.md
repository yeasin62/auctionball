# Bidding from your phone

This guide is for **team owners and captains** who need to place bids during the live auction. Read this before auction night so you're not figuring it out under pressure.

## Two ways to get to your bidding screen

### Option 1 — Use the link your organizer shared with you

Your tournament organizer sent you a link that looks like `auctionball.com/join/abc123…`. Open it on your phone. That's your bidding screen — simple, no login.

Tap the **share icon → Add to Home Screen** so you can open it with one tap on auction night. The link works only for your team — anyone with the link can bid on your behalf, so don't share it publicly.

### Option 2 — Log in with your own account

If your organizer invited you with an email, follow the link in your email, set a password, and log in.

After login, click **Place bids** in the sidebar — that's the only page you'll see. No admin tools, no other distractions.

## What you'll see

- **Top header** — your team name, your team's short code, and your **remaining budget** (always visible)
- A green / amber / red dot — your **internet connection** status
- A pill saying RUNNING, PAUSED, or IDLE — what the auction is doing right now
- The **player card** — photo, name, position, base price
- The **current bid** and the **timer**
- A big **BID button** at the bottom

## How to place a bid

1. Wait for the status pill to say **RUNNING**
2. The amount the BID button shows is the **smallest valid next bid** — the current bid plus the bid step (set by your organizer; e.g., ৳5,000)
3. Tap **+** if you want to bid more than the minimum (each tap adds one bid step)
4. Tap **−** if you want to lower it back (you can't go below the minimum)
5. Tap the big **BID** button to send your bid

The big screen and the auctioneer's panel update within a second. If you're now leading, you'll see "**You're leading**" on your phone in green.

## Anti-snipe rule (important)

If your bid lands in the **last 10 seconds** of the timer, the timer automatically extends to 10 seconds. This is to make the auction fair — nobody can sneak a winning bid in the final half-second.

If you bid right at the buzzer, expect the timer to keep going for another 10 seconds. Don't think your bid was rejected — it was accepted, and now everyone has a chance to counter.

## Why was my bid rejected?

You'll see a red error message under the BID button if your bid didn't go through. Common reasons:

| Error | Why | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| "Auction is not running" | The auctioneer hasn't started the timer yet | Wait for them to click Start |
| "Bid must be at least ৳X" | Someone bid right before you and the minimum jumped up | Tap **+** to update your amount and try again |
| "Bid exceeds team budget" | You don't have enough money left | You're tapped out for this player |
| "Bid window closed" | The timer hit zero just as you tapped | Too late — wait for the next player |

## A few tips

- **Use airplane mode → enable Wi-Fi only** if your venue's mobile data is unreliable. Wi-Fi tends to be more stable in halls.
- **Don't stare at your phone** — keep one eye on the big screen so you can see the room reacting and read the auctioneer's body language.
- **Plan your max bid before each player.** Don't bid in the heat of the moment unless you've already decided what they're worth to you.
- **Vibration feedback** — if your phone supports it, you'll feel a small buzz when you submit, when you take the lead, when you're outbid, and when a player is sold.

## What if I have trouble during the auction?

Wave to your organizer. They can place a bid on your behalf from the auction control panel — just tell them which team you are and how much.
