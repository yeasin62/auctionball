# Creating your first season

A "season" is one tournament — one auction event with its own teams, players, and budgets. You can have several seasons (paid plans), but only one is **active** at a time. The active season is the one your live auction page shows.

## Make a new season

1. Click **Seasons** in the left sidebar
2. Click the blue **+ New season** button at the top right
3. Fill in:

| Field | What it means | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Name** | What you want to call this tournament | "BPL 2026 Spring Cup" |
| **Year** | Defaults to this year | 2026 |
| **Sport** | Cricket or football. Changes the player fields automatically | Cricket |
| **Budget per team** | How much money each team can spend at the auction | ৳5,00,000 |
| **Bid step (BDT)** | The smallest amount a new bid must beat the current bid by, in BDT | 1,000 |
| **Bid step (USD)** | Same, but if you run the auction in USD mode | 10 |
| **Start date** | When the tournament starts (just for display) | 1 May 2026 |
| **End date** | When it ends (just for display) | 15 May 2026 |

4. Click **Save**

You'll see your new season in the list.

## Make it the "active" season

Only one season at a time can drive the live auction. To switch:

- On the seasons list, find the one you want to use
- Click the **Set active** button next to it
- The previous active season is now inactive — its data stays exactly where it is, you can come back to it later

You'll see a green **ACTIVE** badge next to the new active season.

## Auto-finalize: a useful toggle

When the bid timer hits zero, what should happen?

- **Auto-finalize ON** (this is the default) — if there's a leading bid, the player is sold automatically. If nobody bid, the player is marked unsold. The auctioneer doesn't have to click anything.
- **Auto-finalize OFF** — the lot stays open after the timer runs out. The auctioneer clicks **Sold** or **Unsold** manually whenever they're ready.

You can flip this toggle any time on the live auction page. Most organizers leave it on.

## What about the bid step?

The bid step is the minimum amount a new bid must beat the current one by. If the current bid is ৳1,00,000 and your bid step is ৳5,000, the next bid must be ৳1,05,000 or higher.

We give you two separate bid steps — one for BDT mode (e.g., 1000) and one for USD mode (e.g., 10) — so the numbers stay round in both currencies. There's no automatic conversion between them; pick what feels right for your auction.

## Editing a season later

Click any season in the list to edit its name or bid steps. You can't change a season's sport after it's created (because that would invalidate the players already in it).
