# Adding teams

Teams are the buyers in your auction. Every team starts with the same budget (the **Budget per team** you set on the season) and spends it as they win players.

You have two ways to add teams: type them in yourself, or share a sign-up link and let team owners register on their own.

## Option A — Add a team manually

This is fastest if you only have a handful of teams and you already have their info.

1. Click **Teams** in the left sidebar
2. Click **+ New team** at the top right
3. Fill in:

| Field | What it is | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Team name** | What the team is called | "Dhaka Dynamites" |
| **Short code** | A 3-letter abbreviation shown on the big screen | "DHK" |
| **Owner / captain name** | The person in charge of the team | "Md. Karim Ahmed" |
| **Logo** | Square team badge (we crop it to 400×400) | Upload a PNG or JPG |
| **Initial budget** | Optional — defaults to the season's budget. Set a different amount only if this team should start with more or less | ৳5,00,000 |

4. Click **Save**

Repeat for each team.

## Option B — Let teams sign up themselves

This works well when you have 8+ teams or when you don't have all their details yet.

1. Click **Teams** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **Public registration** button at the top
3. Turn the toggle **on**
4. Fill in (optional):
   - **Registration fee** — how much each team pays to join your tournament
   - **Instructions** — anything you want teams to read before they sign up (your bKash number, deadline, rules)
5. Copy the **public link** that appears (it looks like `auctionball.com/tr/abc123`)
6. Share the link with team owners — WhatsApp, Facebook, email, anywhere

Each team owner clicks the link, fills in their team info, pays your fee (if any), and submits. Their team shows up in your **Pending** list for you to review.

## Approving (or rejecting) pending teams

When you have public registration on, teams don't go straight into the auction — you decide who's in.

1. Click **Teams** — at the top you'll see **Pending: X**
2. Click any pending team to see their details (name, captain, transaction ID, logo)
3. Click **Approve** to add them to your tournament, or **Reject** if something's wrong

Once approved, they count toward your plan's team limit and they can be assigned in the auction.

## Editing a team later

Click any team in the list to update:

- Name, short code, captain
- Logo (you can replace or remove it)
- Initial budget — useful if the team paid more / less than others

If you edit the budget after the auction has started, the team's "remaining" budget is adjusted by the same amount.

## Linking a team owner to a real account

If you want a team owner to log in with their own AuctionBall account (instead of just opening a shared link), invite them as a "Team owner":

1. Go to **Users** in the sidebar
2. Click **Invite**
3. Pick role **Team owner**, choose their team, type their email
4. They get an email, click the link, set a password, and log in

When they log in, they only see one page: their team's bidding screen. They can't access anything else.
