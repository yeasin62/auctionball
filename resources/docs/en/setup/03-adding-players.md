# Adding players

Players are what teams bid on. Every player has a starting (base) price and goes through the auction in this order:

> in queue → currently being auctioned → sold or unsold

## Option A — Add players one by one

Best when you have a small list (under 30) and want to type them in yourself.

1. Click **Players** in the left sidebar
2. Click **+ Add player** at the top right
3. Fill in:

| Field | What it is | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Name** | The player's full name | Shakib Rahman |
| **Photo** | Headshot (we crop it to 300×300) | Upload a JPG or PNG |
| **Base price** | Starting price for the bidding | ৳50,000 |
| **Category** | Group players by skill — Elite, Regular, or New | Elite |
| **Position** | Their playing role | All-rounder |
| **Jersey number** | The number on their kit (optional) | 7 |
| **Profession** | Their day job (optional, just for the audience) | Software engineer |
| **Batting style** | Right-hand bat / Left-hand bat (cricket only) | Right-hand bat |
| **Bowling style** | Spin / pace style (cricket only) | Right-arm off-spin |

4. Click **Save**

## Option B — Import from Excel or CSV

Best when you have 30+ players already in a spreadsheet.

1. Click **Players** → **Import** at the top
2. Click **Download template** to get a CSV file with the right columns
3. Open the template in Excel or Google Sheets, fill in your players (one per row), save as CSV
4. Click **Choose file**, pick your CSV, and click **Import**

If any rows have errors (missing name, invalid number, etc.), we'll show you a list — fix them and try again.

## Option C — Let players register themselves

Best when you want players to fill in their own details + pay a registration fee.

1. Click **Seasons** in the sidebar → click **Public registration** on your active season
2. Turn the toggle **on**, set your registration fee
3. (Recommended) Open the **Form builder** to add custom fields you care about — district, contact, payment proof, etc. See [Customizing the registration form](/help/registration-form-builder)
4. Copy the public link (`auctionball.com/r/abc123…`) and share it with players

Submitted players show up in **Pending** for you to approve before they enter the auction queue.

## Player categories — how to use them

Categories don't change the auction logic; they're just for sorting. Common patterns:

- **Elite** — A+ players, marquee names, foreign signings
- **Regular** — your main pool of players
- **New** — first-tournament / uncapped players

The big screen and rosters page sort and group by category.

## Moving players around

You can move a player between statuses (queue → sold → unsold → queue) any time. Click any player in the list to edit them.

This is handy when:

- A sold player drops out and you want to re-auction them
- An unsold player becomes available later (someone canceled, you want to bring them back)
- You're rehearsing the auction and want to reset everything

When you reset a player, any bids on them are cleared and the buying team's budget is restored.
